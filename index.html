Why Choose Provenance?

Built different. Built better.

100% Open Source

Fully transparent code on GitHub. No hidden tracking, no data collection, no mystery code. Contribute, audit, or fork - it's your freedom.

Premier Emulator on Apple TV

Experience retro gaming on the big screen. Native tvOS support with TopShelf integration brings your games to the living room like no other app can.

38+ Systems in One App

From Atari 2600 to PlayStation 2, including rare systems like PC-FX and Virtual Boy. Nearly all CD-based cores support CHD disc images, including a custom CHD implementation in our native Mednafen core not found in other standalone Mednafen forks.

Free Forever (Sideloaded)

When you sideload or build from source, everything is free. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no tiers. App Store builds include optional Provenance Plus features, but all core functionality remains free.

Actively Developed

10+ years of active development with new features shipping regularly. Recent additions include online cheat code lookup from the libretro database, an in-app controller guide, expanded cheat support across 10+ cores, and configurable CRT shaders.

Museum-Quality Library

Not just a game list - a digital collection. Automatic artwork, manuals, release info. Your ROMs deserve better presentation.

Your Games, Beautifully Presented

Every game gets the treatment it deserves. Automatic artwork downloads, release information, ratings, and even scanned manuals. Edit any detail to make your library truly yours.

Customize emulation settings in real-time. Dial in configurable CRT scanline and phosphor shaders, adjust audio, tweak per-core options, and look up cheat codes directly from the libretro database — all without leaving your game.
