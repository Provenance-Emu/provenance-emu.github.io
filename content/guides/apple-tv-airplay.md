Provenance is one of the only iOS emulators with native Apple TV support, letting you play 38+ retro game systems on your television. This guide covers both the native tvOS app and using AirPlay to mirror from your iPhone or iPad.

## Option 1: Native tvOS App

The Provenance tvOS app runs directly on Apple TV, giving you the best performance and lowest latency. This is the recommended approach for regular gaming sessions.

### Steps

1. **Install Provenance on your Apple TV.** Open the App Store on your Apple TV, search for "Provenance," and download it.

2. **Add games to Provenance.** The easiest way is to use the Provenance app on your iPhone or iPad to add games, then use a shared library or WebDAV to sync them. You can also add ROMs via iTunes File Sharing from a Mac or PC.

3. **Pair a controller.** The Apple TV Siri Remote provides basic input but is not ideal for gaming. Pair a Bluetooth controller via **Apple TV Settings > Remotes and Devices > Bluetooth**. See our [controller setup guide](/guides/controller-setup/) for details.

4. **Launch Provenance on Apple TV.** Open the app, navigate to your game library, and select a game to play.

5. **Adjust display settings if needed.** If the screen does not fill your TV, check your Apple TV display settings under **Settings > Video and Audio** and ensure the resolution matches your TV.

## Option 2: AirPlay from iPhone or iPad

If you prefer to run the game on your iPhone or iPad and stream the display to your TV, you can use AirPlay.

### Steps

1. **Connect your Apple TV and iPhone/iPad to the same Wi-Fi network.**

2. **Open Control Center on your iPhone or iPad.** Swipe down from the top-right corner (on Face ID devices) or up from the bottom (on older devices).

3. **Tap Screen Mirroring** and select your Apple TV from the list.

4. **Open Provenance on your iPhone or iPad and launch a game.** The display will mirror to your TV in real time.

5. **Pair a controller to your iPhone or iPad** for a better gaming experience. See our [controller setup guide](/guides/controller-setup/).

## Tips

- For the best gaming experience on Apple TV, use the native tvOS app rather than AirPlay, which can introduce input lag over Wi-Fi.
- A wired Ethernet connection for your Apple TV will reduce any network-related issues.
- The SteelSeries Nimbus+ and Xbox Series X/S controllers are well-tested with the Provenance tvOS app.
- Volume can be adjusted using the Apple TV remote even while a game is running.
