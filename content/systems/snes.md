{{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} brings the Super Nintendo Entertainment System to your iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV. With a library spanning RPGs, platformers, fighting games, and more, the SNES remains one of the most beloved consoles ever made — and now you can experience it all on your Apple device without a jailbreak.

As a free, open-source app available directly from the App Store, {{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} makes it simple to load your own SNES ROM files and start playing within minutes.

## Supported File Formats

{{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} supports the most common SNES ROM formats:

- `.smc` — The most widely used Super Nintendo ROM format
- `.sfc` — Super Famicom ROM files (Japanese releases)
- `.fig` — Early dump format, still widely compatible
- `.swc` — Super Wild Card format

## Features

Playing SNES games in {{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} gives you access to the full suite of emulator features:

- **Save States** — Save and resume at any point, not just official in-game saves
- **Controller Support** — Full MFi, Xbox, and PlayStation controller compatibility; on-screen touch controls also available
- **Shader Support** — CRT scanline and other visual filters to recreate the look of playing on a real TV
- **Cheat Codes** — Game Genie and Pro Action Replay code support
- **Fast Forward** — Speed through slow sections or grinding sequences
- **On-Screen Controls** — Customizable touch overlay for playing without a physical controller

## iCloud Sync with Provenance Plus

[Provenance Plus](/plus/) adds iCloud sync for your SNES save states and game library, so your progress carries across all your devices automatically. If you play on your iPhone during a commute and pick up an Apple TV controller at home, Plus keeps everything in sync.

## How to Get Started

1. **Download {{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}}** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/provenance-app/id1596862805) — it's free.
2. **Add your SNES ROM files** to {{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} using iTunes File Sharing, the Files app, or a web server import.
3. **Start playing** — your game library appears automatically once files are imported.

For detailed setup instructions, visit the [Provenance wiki](https://wiki.provenance-emu.com/).
