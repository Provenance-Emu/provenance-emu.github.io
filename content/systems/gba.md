Provenance includes a full Game Boy Advance emulator for iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV. The GBA had an outstanding lineup of games — Pokemon FireRed and LeafGreen, The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap, Metroid Fusion, Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow — and Provenance lets you play the entire library on your Apple device.

Available free on the App Store with no jailbreak required, Provenance is one of the most capable GBA emulators available for iOS.

## Supported File Formats

- `.gba` — Standard Game Boy Advance ROM format

## Features

- **Save States** — Save and restore your progress at any point, independent of in-game saves
- **Battery Save Emulation** — Full support for GBA game save files, so in-game saves work exactly as expected
- **Controller Support** — Works with MFi, Xbox, and PlayStation wireless controllers; on-screen touch overlay available for handheld play
- **Shader Support** — Pixel-accurate rendering with optional CRT and LCD-style visual filters
- **Cheat Codes** — GameShark and Action Replay code support
- **Fast Forward** — Speed through slow sections or skip repetitive encounters

## iCloud Sync with Provenance Plus

[Provenance Plus]({{< relref "/plus/" >}}) brings iCloud sync to your GBA save states and library. Start a Pokemon run on your iPhone and pick up exactly where you left off on your iPad or Apple TV — automatically.

## How to Get Started

1. **Download Provenance** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/provenance-app/id1596862805) — it's free.
2. **Add your GBA ROM files** using iTunes File Sharing, the Files app, or Provenance's web import.
3. **Start playing** — your Game Boy Advance games appear in your library immediately.

For detailed setup instructions, visit the [Provenance wiki](https://wiki.provenance-emu.com/).
