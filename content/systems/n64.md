Provenance brings the Nintendo 64 to your iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV. The N64 delivered legendary 3D gaming experiences — Super Mario 64, The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, GoldenEye 007, Mario Kart 64, and more — and Provenance makes it possible to revisit those games on your Apple device.

No jailbreak is required, and Provenance is available free on the App Store as an open-source project.

## Supported File Formats

N64 ROMs come in several byte-order variants:

- `.z64` — Big-endian format; the preferred and most compatible format
- `.n64` — Little-endian format
- `.v64` — Byte-swapped format

## Features

- **Save States** — Save anywhere, not just at official in-game checkpoints
- **Controller Support** — Physical controllers (MFi, Xbox, PlayStation) provide the most authentic N64 experience; analog stick input is fully supported; on-screen controls available
- **Shader Support** — Visual enhancement filters for a cleaner modern look or a faithful retro presentation
- **Cheat Codes** — GameShark N64 code support

## iCloud Sync with Provenance Plus

[Provenance Plus]({{< relref "/plus/" >}}) adds iCloud sync for your Nintendo 64 save states. Your progress through Ocarina of Time or a Mario Kart season is automatically backed up and synced across all your devices.

## How to Get Started

1. **Download Provenance** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/provenance-app/id1596862805) — it's free.
2. **Add your N64 ROM files** via iTunes File Sharing, the Files app, or web import. The `.z64` format is recommended for best compatibility.
3. **Start playing** — Provenance will detect and display your Nintendo 64 games.

For detailed setup instructions, visit the [Provenance wiki](https://wiki.provenance-emu.com/).
