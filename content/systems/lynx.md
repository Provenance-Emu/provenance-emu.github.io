The Atari Lynx was the world's first color handheld gaming console, released in 1989 — beating the Game Boy Color to market by almost a decade. Its hardware was technically impressive for its time, featuring hardware-accelerated sprites and scaling. Standout titles include California Games, Chip's Challenge, Rygar, and Blue Lightning.

Provenance emulates the Atari Lynx through the Mednafen core.

## Supported File Formats

- `.lnx` — Standard Atari Lynx ROM format
- `.lyx` — Alternative Lynx ROM extension (headerless format)

## BIOS

The Atari Lynx requires a BIOS file for full compatibility. See [BIOS Requirements](https://wiki.provenance-emu.com/installation-and-usage/bios-requirements) for details.

## Features

- **Mednafen Core** — Accurate Lynx emulation
- **Save States** — Snapshot and restore progress at any point
- **Controller Support** — Full MFi, Xbox, and PlayStation wireless controller support
- **Orientation Support** — Some Lynx games were designed to be rotated 90° — Provenance handles both orientations

## iCloud Sync with Provenance Plus

[Provenance Plus](/plus/) syncs your Lynx save states and library across all your Apple devices via iCloud.

1. **Download Provenance** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/provenance-app/id1596862805) — it's free.
2. **Add your Lynx ROM files** and BIOS via the Files app or Wi-Fi import.
