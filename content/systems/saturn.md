The Sega Saturn was a cult classic — complex hardware that most developers struggled with, but that gave rise to some genuinely extraordinary games. Nights into Dreams, Virtua Fighter 2, Panzer Dragoon Saga, and the Japan-only port of Castlevania: Symphony of the Night all found their home on Saturn. {{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} brings this rare library to your iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV.

{{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} is free, open-source, and available directly from the App Store. No jailbreak required.

## Supported File Formats

Saturn games were CD-ROM based. {{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} supports the standard Saturn disc image formats:

- `.bin` / `.cue` — The most common Saturn dump format; keep both files in the same directory when importing
- `.iso` — Standard disc image format compatible with many Saturn releases
- `.chd` — Compressed Hunks of Data format; highly recommended for Saturn libraries due to the space savings on large disc collections

Multi-disc Saturn games are supported, with disc-switching handled within the emulator.

## Features

Playing Sega Saturn games in {{< brand >}}Provenance{{< /brand >}} gives you access to the full suite of emulator features:

- **Save States** — Save at any point; especially valuable for the Saturn's longer RPGs and adventure games
- **Controller Support** — Full MFi, Xbox, and PlayStation controller compatibility; the Saturn's 6-button layout maps well to modern controllers for fighting games like Virtua Fighter
- **Shader Support** — CRT scanline and visual filters to replicate the look of Saturn games on period-accurate hardware
- **Cheat Codes** — Code support for Saturn titles
- **Fast Forward** — Speed through loading sequences, which were notoriously slow on Saturn hardware
- **On-Screen Controls** — Customizable touch overlay for playing without a physical controller
