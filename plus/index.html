Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get beta access?

Provenance has a public TestFlight beta with limited slots. Patreon supporters get their own dedicated invitation queue with more availability. Beta access is a Patreon benefit, separate from the Plus App Store subscription.

Do I lose my data if I cancel Plus?

No. All your local data (ROMs, saves, settings) stays on your device. You lose the ability to sync across devices, but previously synced data remains on each device where it was downloaded.

Can I use Plus on multiple devices?

Yes. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID. Sign in on any device and your Plus benefits follow you. Plus also supports Family Sharing for up to 6 family members.

Why is Apple TV sync free?

tvOS aggressively reclaims storage from apps, which can delete your ROMs and saves without warning. To protect users from unexpected data loss, iCloud sync is included free and enabled by default on Apple TV.

Is sideloading free forever?

Yes. If you sideload or build from source, all features (including those that are Plus-only on the App Store) are free. Provenance is open source — Plus subscriptions on the App Store support development.

What happens to my synced data if my subscription lapses?

Your data stays on every device it was synced to. You just won't be able to sync new changes until you resubscribe. Nothing is deleted.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Your subscription restores automatically when you sign in with the same Apple ID. You can also go to Settings > Provenance Plus > Restore Purchases in the app.
